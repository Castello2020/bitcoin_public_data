Super Experience - Best with a group of 4!

Friday, May 18, 2012  sQren  

This trick works the same way as the trick we posted yesterday, but this time as a larger group. Once again we are back in Act 3  killing Azmodan on  Nightmare mode. This works on Normal as well, but you get less exp. Here is the run down:

  • Group up with a party of at least 2 people.
  • Lets say you use 4, lets call them P1, P2, P3 and P4.
  • Kill Azmodan, but do NOT complete the quest.
  • P1 is getting boosted first.
  • P2 P3 P4 all leave the game, and create a new game through RESUME GAME.
  • P1 completes both quests leading up to act 4.
  • P1 Leaves his game, and joins P2.
  • P1 and P2 complete both quest leading up to act 4.
  • P1 and P2 join P3 in his game.
  • P1, P2 and P3 repeat the same step...
  • P1 P2 P3 join P4 repeat...
  • Rotate players
Repeat.

This gives up to 360k xp for player 1, 270k for player 2, 180k for player3 and the original 90k for player 1. Rotate this and see the exp explode.

This trick is perfect for boosting  your toons that's not already level 60.
