About Us

The authors, sponsors, and members of the group behind Belligerent Act are financial managers, entrepreneurs, designers, writers, philosophers, and computer/web professionals. We are nonpartisan in the conventional political usage of that term; we are not liberals, not conservatives, not centrists, not democrats, not republicans.

Our estimate of the current and past five U.S. Presidents is as follows:

Barack Obama: SUPERCRIMINAL
George W. Bush: SUPERCRIMINAL
William J. (Bill) Clinton: SUPERCRIMINAL
George H. W. Bush: SUPERCRIMINAL
Ronald Reagan: SUPERCRIMINAL
Jimmy Carter: Anomaly

We do not believe that any of the conventional political dichotomies have any meaning or import. The constantly hyped mainstream struggle between the left and the right is a strategically curated sideshow. The only meaningful schism in America today is that between the financial interests of those in political power vs. the financial interests of those not in political power. In other words, we believe that the only significant political struggle in America today is between the people and the government.

We are attempting to articulate a coherent alternative to the political and social right, the political and social left, and the political and social center (compromise is one of the most insidious tools of the bureaucrat and the status quo). It’s clear to us that all of those paths are tired, discredited, and infected with rot. The only real solutions to our urgent cultural emergency require going meta; charting new paths that break from and transcend all the corruption, inertia, and artificial conflict in traditional politics.
