Genre: Fantasy / Horror
Director: Alejandro Jodorowsky
Starring: Axel Jodorowsky, Blanca Guerra, Guy Stockwell, Thelma Tixou
Duration: 123 min.
Rating: 8.3/10

Summary:
Fenix is the son of a circus strongman and an aerialist. One night, the mother sees from her high perspective that her husband is fooling around with the tattooed lady. She later confronts him and throws acid on him in retaliation. He saws off her arms in return and kills himself. Years later, Fenix is released from an insane asylum by his armless mother. She wants to go on a murderous revenge spree, and maybe play a little piano, and she needs Fenix to be her arms for both tasks.

36th Cesar Awards (2011)


Today, the 36th Annual Cesar Awards will be held at the Theatre du Chatelet in Paris. American actress Jodie Foster is presiding over this year's ceremony and Quentin Tarantino will be receiving an honorary award for his work.  

French speakers can watch it televised online at http://www.canalplus.fr starting at 10AM PST/1PM EST.

Leading the pack is Cannes Grand Prix winner, Xavier Beauvois’ Of Gods and Men with 10 nominations, followed by Roman Polanski's The Ghost Writer, and Joann Sfar's Gainsbourg (Vie héroïque) each with 8 nominations.
