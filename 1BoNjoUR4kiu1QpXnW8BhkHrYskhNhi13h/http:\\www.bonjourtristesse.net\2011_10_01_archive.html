Random Musings - October 2011

Random Notes of the Month

This blog has reached 400 posts.
Happy Halloween folks, another month has gone by and this one marked several milestones at bonjourtristesse.net.

Early on in the month I got my 300th follower, thank you to Castor Troy of Anomalous Material!  Then a couple weeks later I gained my 400th follower on Twitter, and also published my 400th post.

That's not all however.  Midway through October I crossed the 100,000 pageview mark!  I think that's pretty amazing considering the obscure content I generally cover here.  Thank you to all my readers, you guys are great!

I also expected the monthly traffic to take a huge dip after changing the domain name last month, but that wasn't the case.  It was slower for about 10 days and then quickly started picking up again to end up being my busiest month ever, just shy of 18,000 views.

"In Turin on 3rd January, 1889, Friedrich Nietzsche steps out of the doorway of number six, Via Carlo Albert. Not far from him, the driver of a hansom cab is having trouble with a stubborn horse. Nietzsche comes up to the throng and puts an end to the brutal scene, throwing his arms around the horse’s neck, sobbing. His landlord takes him home, he lies motionless and silent for two days on a divan until he mutters the obligatory last words, and lives for another ten years, silent and demented, cared for by his mother and sisters. We do not know what happened to the horse.”
