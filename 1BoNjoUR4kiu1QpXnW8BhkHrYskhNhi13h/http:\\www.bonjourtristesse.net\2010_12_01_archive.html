The 68th Golden Globe Awards (2011) - Best Foreign Language Film

 Back on December 14th, the Hollywood Foreign Press Association announced the nominees for this year's Golden Globe Awards that will be held on Sunday, January 16, 2011 starting at 8 PM EST at the Beverly Hilton Hotel.     

The five official nominees for the 2011 Best Foreign Language Film award are:

In A Better World / Hævnen - Denmark (Winner)
Director: Susanne Bier
Zentropa Entertainment; Sony Pictures Classics

Biutiful - Mexico, Spain
Director: Alejandro González Iñárritu
Menageatroz, Focus Features International; Roadside Attractions

The Concert - France
Director: Radu Mihaileanu
An Oï Oï Oï Productions, Les Productions Du Tresor, France 3 Cinema, Europacorp, Castel Films, Panache Productions, RTBF (Belgian Television), BIM Distrubuzione Co., Canal +, Cinecinema and France 3; The Weinstein Company

The Edge / Kpaй - Russia
Director: Aleksei Uchitel
Channel One Russia; Samuel Goldwyn (Russia)

I Am Love / Io Sono l’Amore - Italy
Director: Luca Guadagnino
First Sun; Magnolia Pictures 

The rest of the nominees for the 68th Annual Golden Globe Awards can be found here.
