How to communicate with your friends from Windows Live Messenger to Skype



Now users of the Microsoft Messenger can easily get in touch with your friends through Skype. In its version 6.0 for Mac and Windows the opportunity to enter into the program, using the data from the
account Microsoft.



Messenger users enough to download the latest
version of Skype, and all their friends and colleagues are already in your contact list. "We want to focus our efforts on making things easier for our
customers and at the same time - to constantly
improve the overall experience of their use," - says
the president of the Microsoft Skype Tony Bates
(Tony Bates). According to him, the company will
cease to support the Messenger in the first quarter
of 2013 in all countries except China - where the
program is still available.

Clicking on Skype, users will receive a number of new features, including:

- Support for a wide range of devices, including the iPad and tablets based on Android;
- Instant messaging, video calls, calls to landline and mobile phones via a single application;
- The opportunity to show his screen interlocutor;
- Video calls on mobile phones;
- Connect with your friends on Facebook via video calls;
- Group video.

Here is a quick start guide Messenger, wishing to
communicate in Skype:

So, to transfer your contacts to VoIP-program, you
must first download it and run. On the login page,
select the option to login on the account Microsoft.
After that, the program will ask whether you have an
account in Skype. Users of both services can easily
combine both accounts.

After completion of the installation in Skype can see
immediately contact your friends. Clicking on the list,
you can display contacts from only one service. Start
chat with any of the contacts can be one click.
The developers promise that the next few months
will be working hard to make the transition easier for
users Messenger Skype. To enable them to learn
more about VoIP-application, as early as this year to
send special needs.

By the way, Skype client pre-installed on smartphones running Windows Phone 8, and is available for download in the app store for Windows 8.
