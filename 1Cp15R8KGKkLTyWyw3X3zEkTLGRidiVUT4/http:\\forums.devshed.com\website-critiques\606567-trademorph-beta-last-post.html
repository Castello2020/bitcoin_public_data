    * TradeMorph [BETA]


    Code:
    www.trademorph.com
    TradeMorph is proud to introduce the new five-pronged approach to the market that will revolutionize the way individuals will buy, sell and trade. It is a user friendly interface that will create an era of harmony between consumers and advertisers. There will be no need for pop up ads or undesired spam emails. With our innovative website, it will be possible for advertisers to find users who actually want their products, and for consumers to quickly acquire items that they desire. The website is based on recent survey of the marketplace. TradeMorph believes that there are some items you should buy and trade, and other items that you should obtain free. We hope you enjoy your time on the website and spread the word about the new way of marketing to all of your friends, family, and co-workers.

    Buy, Sell, Auction
    The �Buy(Desired)� items on TradeMorph will enable consumers to list products that they desire. Advertisers and other vendors will approach you, the consumer, to talk about their products thus leaving you stress free from using complicated websites, while also saving you time and energy. PayPal and MoneyBookers are just some of the ways offered by TradeMorph to purchases their desired items.

    TradeBot
    The "TradeBot", a never before seen feature, allows the consumers to list virtual commodities that they wish to trade as well as items that they wish to acquire in exchange. TradeBot will notify you, on your welcome page, when a compatible trade is found. An Autotrade link will give you a chance to finalize the exchange. All virtual trading items will be verified to the fullest extent possible.

    Giveaway Manager
    The "Giveaway Master" feature will allow generous and gracious members to provide users with free commodities (invites, accounts, cd-keys, etc) in a safe and simple format. TradeMorph ensures that "Giveaway Master" products will be distributed in a fair manner allowing for �spreading of the wealth� throughout all of our users. The generosity of gracious donators will be rewarded by earning points allowing for purchases of invites and reputation points. Donators can also cash in their tokens for monetary prizes.
