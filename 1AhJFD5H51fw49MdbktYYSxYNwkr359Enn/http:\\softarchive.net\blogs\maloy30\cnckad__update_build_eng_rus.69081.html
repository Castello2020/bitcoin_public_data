cncKad 9.5.177 + update build 198 (Eng/Rus) | 524.54 Mb

Metalix presents the complete CAD/CAM solution for Sheet Metal manufacturing.The Complete CAD/CAM Solution
cncKad is the only software which has integrated CAD/CAM capabilities in the same module: Geometry, Dimensions and Technology (Punching/Cutting) are completely associative - when the Geometry is modified, Dimensions and Technology are automatically updated!
cncKad features 2D & 3D design, Automatic Nesting, NC Generation, Graphic Simulation and Machine Comunication.
Import and Export - 3D integration with Solid Works, SolidEdge, Autodesk Inventor and ProE, through the associative link, and read files directly from AutoCAD.
cncKad fully compatible with Windows Vista and Office 2007




The software cncKad яввляется the integrated system covering a full cycle of operations ЧПУ also includes following modules:

Plotting – effective and at the same time simple in use two-dimensional (2D) the drawing module supporting in addition to a totality of usual drawing tools special auxiliary means for work with sheet metal, including: creation of dredging, facets, interfaces; automatic detection and correction of not closed contours, recognition of forms, check of drawings and use True-type of fonts.

Processing – automatic and interactive graphic processing for technologies of punching, and also laser and plasma are sharp, autogenous and water abrasive processing, including: automatic репозицию, avoiding of clips, processing on strips, sheet cutting off, support of tools of family Wilson Wheel, minimisation of rotation of the tool and creation of the entire accounts. This module supports also automatic punching and резку, use of special tools, репозиции, joint раскрои and so on.

AutoNest – an automatic apportion of details of difficult forms for optimum use of a material, by means of such functions and-or tools as interactivity, grouping, a grill, filling of apertures, creation repeating нестингов, automatic transfer DXF, creation of the entire accounts.

The post-processor – post-processors generate the effective codes including subroutines (Macroes), optimisation of a trajectory of moving of the tool, minimisation of rotation of a revolver of the machine tool, and also support of such functions of the machine tool as greasing, vacuum and the control of speed of punching (ram-rate).

Simulation – Graphic simulation of work allows to edit with ease codes УП during viewing of results of processing.It is automatically checked on presence of errors, such as: absent parametres, problems with clips, an exit for table limits and so on.
The option allows to transform ready codes again to drawings and to feign their processing.

DNC – module DNC supports sending of files in the controller of the machine tool and their reception, and also supports порционную data transmission and extraction of ready codes УП from the machine tool controller.

OS: Windows NT/2000/XP/Vista
