MY BLOGS & COMPILATION

SA F.A.Q. Blog


SA Tutorial - How to use COMPILATION and MY BLOGS.

1. COMPILATION

Compilation - it's compilation from your favorite blogs.

Just Open any Blog - If u like the blog and want to see the articles from this Blog in your compilation - Click [connect].

For example - http://softarchive.net/blogs/faq/



This way you can connect to many Blogs you wants. All article from Connected Blogs will be seen in your Compilation

If you are a regular Visitor of SA, You do not need to open your Favorite Blogs every day. All article from your favorite Blogs will be available in your Compilation.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SA now has a lot of PRIVATE blogs, the question arises: What if I don't want to see them in my COMPILATION but I want to have access to them at any time.

It's easy!

1) Open "MY BLOGS"



2) Find a BLOG which you want to remove from COMPILATION, but want to have access to this blog (without [connect] / [disconnect] every time)

Example with blog: "Nice blog"



As seen in the screenshot above: Blog is PRIVATE, so i want to remove this blog from my COMPILATION and still have access to this blog!

Click on checkbox "Compilation"



Result:



Done! Blog "Nice blog" has been removed from your COMPILATION, but you still have full access to blog.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------



2. MY BLOGS

MY BLOGS - it's page where I can manage ALL my blogs (To which i'm connected)



Q: What does it mean "(This blog have not updated more than 1 week)"?
A: It's mean: this blog have not updated (no new articles) more than a week. Perhaps this blog is "dead". As seen in the screenshot above: blogs "Goodshare" and "The Best Free EBooks" have [Disconnect] link. I want to delete them from my blogs - click to [Disconnect] link for blog "Goodshare" and "The Best Free EBooks", DONE!
Feature allows you remove all "dead" blogs from your page - "MY BLOGS".

Q: What does it mean "+27 or +5"?
A: Numbers show quantity of NEW articles in blog per last 24 hours.


We hope you appreciate all of this.
