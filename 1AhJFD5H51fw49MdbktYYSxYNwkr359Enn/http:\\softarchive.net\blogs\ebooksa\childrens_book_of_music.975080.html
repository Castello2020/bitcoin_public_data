Children's Book Of Music
Reading level: Ages 7 and up | DK Publishing | September 20, 2010 | English | ISBN: 0756667348 | PDF | 144 pages | 43 MB


Music has always been an amazing way for people to connect with each other and to express their ideas and emotions. In this clear and lively introduction to music history, readers take a chronological journey around the world, exploring the sounds and rhythms of different cultures, the development of instruments, and the progression of musical styles. From the beginnings of music for ritual or magical purposes to the technological developments that have taken modern music in new directions, children will discover the fascinating history of music and how it has become an international exchange of ideas.
