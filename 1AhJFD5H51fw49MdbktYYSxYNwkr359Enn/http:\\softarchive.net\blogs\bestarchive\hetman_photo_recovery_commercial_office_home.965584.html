Hetman Photo Recovery 3.1 Commercial / Office / Home | 5 Mb

With Hetman Photo Recovery, the process of recovering digital photos becomes simple and can be performed by anyone. The software recovers lost photos from camera memory cards (Compact Flash, xD, Memory Stick), various HDD and USB drives, mobile phone cards and other sources. You lost files will be recovered even if the camera memory card or a logical drive has been formatted or there has been a hardware crash that resulted in a loss of files. Thanks to a new algorithm of advanced disk analysis, the software recovers images that other tools don't even detect! The trial version allows you to preview deleted images. The photos that you can preview will be 100% recoverable after you register the program!

Every owner of a digital camera, regardless of his or her skills and experience, may face the problem of digital photo recovery. Hetman Photo Recovery is the simplest and most efficient solution of this problem. This software will be equally appreciated by both novices and professional photographers, photo artists and designers. This software combines the ability to perform deep scanning of logical drives, preview and recover photos in the RAW format with a clear and intuitive recovery wizard, which will help even novices recover digital images in no time.

A great number of digital cameras store images in the RAW format to preserve the original picture quality. Our program can preview and recover RAW formats of all popular cameras. This feature will be especially appreciated by professional photo artists. Hetman Photo Recovery was developed as an "image recovery wizard". All you have to do is to select the necessary disk and the program will scan it and show the thumbnails of recoverable images. You can use zooming to preview these images prior to recovering them.

MAIN FEATURES:
» Support of the most popular media cards, various USB and HDD devices, photo cameras and etc
» Support of major file systems: VFAT, FAT12/16/32, NTFS/NTFS4/NTFS5
» You can preview and select images to be recovered
» Support of major types of digital images: *.bmp, *.cel, *.ico, *.jpe, *.jpeg, *.jpg, *.png, *.psd, *.tga, *.tif, *.tiff, *.wmf and etc
» Support of the RAW format: *.032, *.3fr, *.bmq, *.cr2, *.crw, *.cs1, *.dc2, *.dcr, *.raw, *.rdc, *.sr2, *.srf, *.x3f and etc
» The recovery process is fast and simple
