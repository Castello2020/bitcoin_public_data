Rules for the Free

Here's some rules for the free (I know, that sounds weird, RULES for the free).  I think I was inspired partly by things I read about SERE and Army doctrine about how to conduct yourself if you become a POW, and partly by Harry Browne - but I don't own his books, I am just sort of going from memory and my own gut.

Do not tell your business to busybodies, clergy, cops, or shrinks, ever.  (Clergy have also been hijacked in many denominations to be snitches).  As the economy collapses and we get more and more snitches, it will become important to learn how to test someone's trustworthiness; but the less said the better in most cases.  Do not tell people in one part of your life about other parts of your life. i.e. coworkers about family, friends about work, etc. (and here I am blogging, oh well)

Learn how professional liars like politicians, media and sales people, and sociopaths push people's buttons so you recognize it when they are playing you.  Learn Alinsky's disinformation tactics.  Learn the Agenda 21 meeting-hijacking tactics that were developed by the Rand Corporation originally.  Learn NLP.  Learn the tactics of persuasion. You will begin to see the "Fnords" this way. (PS: google "fnord" for a laugh).  Also, learn police/detective interrogation tactics so that you can resist them.
  4. Stop caring what other people think of you, especially if you don't think much of them.  Do not base your identity on some image you "need" to project.

Read instead of watching television.  Modern TV and movies are designed to hypnotize you and plant ideas in your subconscious.  Even down to the flicker rate on the TV screen, which changes your brain waves.  Even radio like NPR does it: using an unsavory story like child sexual abuse, right after one they want you to dislike, like about guns or some conservative they don't like, with no discernable pause or change in tone between the stories.
  6. Work as self employed. Get paid in cash so it doesn't go through a bank.  You want to deprive banks of any knowledge of you or power over you such as being able to freeze your account.
  7. Pay cash as much as you can, to avoid paper trails.  Or pay in other fungibles if you can, so as to avoid dollars.
  8. Give out fake information at stores, like when you're buying stuff and they ask for your address etc.
  9. Don't eat that processed poison that masquerades as food at the supermarket.  Learn to cook from scratch.  Grow or gather your own organic food to the extent that you can. Avoid paying big corporations for food. Find a farmers market, or a farmer.
  10. Learn DIY medicine and practice good natural health/prevention so you stay out of the doctor's office.
  11. Make it expensive for anyone to spy on you - use encryption for everything you can.  Or create a bunch of garbage data for people to sort through so it confuses them.

  14. Pay the very least in taxes that you can get away with.  (Don't live visibly above your apparent means though.)
  15. Don't buy new things.  Buy used things, under the table, whenever possible, or make things yourself. This deprives big corporations of profits and avoids sales taxes.
  16. Do not accept government assistance of any kind.
  17. Keep your money out of banks and get/stay out of debt.  Deprive them of interest payments that enslave.
  18. Keep your money out of the dollar. i.e. buy real estate, gold, silver, guns, other collectibles that you can touch, and don't keep it in money form like the stock market, CD's, bank accounts etc.  It's ok to collect coins, those are made by the mint for the US Treasury instead of the Fed, but if you're going to keep coins, keep nickels and pre-1982 pennies because they're worth more than their face value.
