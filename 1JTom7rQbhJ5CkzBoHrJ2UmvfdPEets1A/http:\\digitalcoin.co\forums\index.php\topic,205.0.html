Cgminer and Cudaminer Guide
     
Most sites have a how to guide but explain little to nothing at all like creating your bat launch file and such.
I will not be covering how to add Cpu miner with proxy until i understand it fully my self, so Cpu miner guide: Coming soon!!

First thing your must do is make a wallet.
Best site for DGC trading and a wallet in my opinion is coinex.pw
Make go to account balances go in find DGC then click Generate this will be your wallet code for DGC remember this.


Step 1. Choosing your pooling site.
I prefer http://www.digicoinpool.com/

Step 2. Signup.
1.Then go to the Dashboard then hover till you see my account then click My Workers.

2.On the page you should see this.


3. set your worker name and pass to what you want you will need it for the miner.

Step 3. Choosing your miner.
1. Cgminer best for amd users: http://tinyurl.com/3pnty2z

or

2. Cudaminer best for Nvidia requires no setting added it will auto setup on start:http://tinyurl.com/md3zts7

Step 4. Extract your Miner into your download folder.

Step 5. Time to create your bat file.
1.Right click on your desktop then make a new text document, -When it asks for a name make it launcher.bat instead of .txt -

2. Right click on the .bat file open it up and place this code inside You will need to change Otakusama to your computer profile name.
Cuderminer bat script:
Code: Copy under here.
c:
cd \Users\Otakusama\Downloads\cudaminer
cudaminer -u WorkerUser -p workerpass -o stratum+tcp://pool.digicoinpool.com:3344
pause

Or for amd users.

Cgminer bat script: The place your see --scrypt you need to find the scrypt for your card, can be found here
http://bit.ly/1cSSoHU To make it easier on your i placed an = were to paste your code.
Code: Copy under here.
c:
cd \Users\Otakusama\Downloads\cgminer-3.6.4
cgminer --scrypt = -u WorkerUser -p workerpass -o stratum+tcp://pool.digicoinpool.com:3344
pause


Step 4. You should be to go! Just click start on your launcher.bat

"Blocks" are transaction IDs (TXID). In essence, synchronizing with the blockchain is like downloading a file via torrent. Once fully synchronized (aka becoming a seed) all TXIDs are available on your computer. Unlike a torrent, the blockchain is constantly growing and updating, thus it needs to synchronize each time the wallet is opened.

Which miner software are you using?

Digitalcoin.conf is a file you create on your own and put into the APPDATA/Roaming/Digitalcoin folder. It basically adds nodes to the network, or allows for solo mining.
