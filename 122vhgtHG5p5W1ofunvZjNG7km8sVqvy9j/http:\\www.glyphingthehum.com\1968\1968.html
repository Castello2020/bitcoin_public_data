1968 Timeline

1968 was the year that changed everything.  From technology and pop culture to the horrific assasinations of MLK and RFK, the world was never the same after the events of this year.  It was also the year I was born.

I invested hundreds of hours preparing the source material, editing the graphics, and learning to code Sliverlight in order to create this resource.  If you get value from it, please consider tossing me a few Satoshis at: 122vhgtHG5p5W1ofunvZjNG7km8sVqvy9j

Tips for looking at the timeline:

  • Allow the silverlight Active-X control to run on your browser.

  • Try using the Full Screen option of the browser.  This is the F11 key for most popular browsers.

  • Adjust your browser zoom level.  You may need to go down to 85% or so to fit the whole thing in one window without scrolling vertically.

  • Use the icons on the left to move between Music, Politics, Births and Deaths, Science and Pop Culture.  Enjoy!
