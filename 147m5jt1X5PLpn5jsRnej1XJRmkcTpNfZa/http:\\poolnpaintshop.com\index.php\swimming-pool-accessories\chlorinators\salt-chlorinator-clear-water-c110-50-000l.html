Description

Clear Water C110 Chlorinator for pools of 50,000 litres.

Sanitising of swimming pools by Salt Chlorination is the safest, most effective and most environmentally friendly method of all. Clearwater Salt Chlorinators automatically generate the right chlorine level necessary to keep your pool free of bacteria and algae. The result is healthier water that feels great and looks great too. Enjoy the pleasure of having a pool without the need for overzealous pool cleaning with our Clearwater Salt Chlorinators. By installing the Clearwater C Series Salt Chlorinator you'll be able to save the time and money that you would have spent adding chlorine to your pool each day. This Salt Chlorinator automatically adjusts chlorine levels and is the perfect entry level salt chlorinator.

Features
- Chlorine meter and output control.
- Easy installation.
- Suits new and existing pools.
- High chlorine output to cater for most pool sizes.
- 1 Year manufacturers warranty
- 36 month manufacturing defects warranty.
