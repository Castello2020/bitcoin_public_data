      Hi everyone!

      This is my first post. I am considering buying a new Lumia 920,my first WP8 device. At first, Lumia 925 was my number one choice. The price is not a problem. I like 925's design(although I still prefer 920's), it's slimmer and lighter than 920 but it won't be a problem. I would not need wireless charging and the display difference is not something I would care about since I am an average user. Everything else is the same but internal storage. Since I don't much use my present phone, I wasn't sure if 16GB(13GB to be precise) would be enough for me. So 32GB is something that would surely do for a long time(I want to be able to take a lot of photos and videos, have my favorite music and apps). The cameras on these two phones are very similar(Lumia 925 takes sharper photos but I don't really care), especially after Amber update. So, I finally decided to buy Lumia 920.

      Is now good time to buy Lumia 920? My main concern is for how long will it be able to support apps(I hope for 2-3 years since Windows Phone 8 is less than one year old). I'd like to use this phone 2 years. We still don't have Lumia 1020 in Serbia and it will be too costly at the time it goes on sale so I haven't even thought about it. I think it will be able to support apps for at least 2 years since Lumia 925 is new phone at it has the same specs as Lumia 920.

      Thanks

      I have a 920 and don't ever use the wireless charging, like you i dont consider it a selling point. If i was given a free charging plate then i'd definitely use it, but i'm not fortunate enough and wouldn't spend $20+ for one.

      With that said, the 32GB is a huge advantage. I'm not the type of user to require TONS of storage, but if you become a picture happy person like myself you'll be thankful for the additional space.

      Like some have said, I do love my 920 BUT i'd consider waiting out for the new flagship phones which we should expect an announce in a few months. I am sure the 920 will hold up for the next 2 years, however since Windows 8.1/Blue is being totted as a major leap in the OS its possible updates later on may not be supported.

      Would i sign a 2 year contract right now on a L920? It's hard for me to jump and say yes considering what could be around the corner.

      Is the 920 an excellent device? Yes

      My input: Buy a 520/521 for now to use and wait out the upcoming phones to renew your contract
