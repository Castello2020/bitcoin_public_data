CANBUS Fault Codes - For Vauxhall Corsa, Astra, Vectra etc

These codes are used for diagnostic fault finding on vehicles which use the CANBUS system.
My car is a Vauxhall Astra MK4 - these codes have helped me diagnose problems with my car.

 There are various ways to obtain these codes from your vehicle. On Vauxhall Astra's made 1998-2000, the Paperclip Method can be used as shown in this video:
Other vehicles may have similar methods, Google is your friend here...
