RandomControl Arion MaxLive v2.0.3 – XFORCE

RandomControl LIVE is the technology the brings our hybrid rendering engine right into Autodesk 3D Studio MAX and Softimage.

LIVE eliminates the need for a stand-alone rendering application by tightly embedding our engine in the host. This way, all scene properties such as materials and cameras are native to the host application and it becomes possible to seamlessly render animations in the host’s framebuffer, use the host’s network render manager, and more.
