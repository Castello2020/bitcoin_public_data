PENN Mock Trial

Welcome to the Penn Mock Trial website! The Penn mock trial team is open to all undergraduates. We are proud to be home to a wide array of students, from pre-law to computer science majors! Try-outs are held in the beginning of fall semester, click on the join button to find out more.

We are also proud to host two tournaments throughout the year: The college mock trial quaker classic invitation in the fall and the High School mock trial Benjamin Franklin Invitational in the Winter.
