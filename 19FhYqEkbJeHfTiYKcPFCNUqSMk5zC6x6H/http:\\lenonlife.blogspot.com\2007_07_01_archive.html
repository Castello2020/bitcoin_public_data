Sunday, July 29, 2007

hiking or biking

Current music: Black Heart Procession - The Spell

Stayed in last night in order to get up early this morning and go hiking at Mt. McConnell northwest of Fort Collins. Of course I set the time on my alarm clock, but didn't turn the alarm on. Instead of waking at 6, it was 8. Damn, no time to drive all the way there if I am still going to play kickball. I have to play because our game is against Science for the People. Decided to go for a nice bike ride up to Cherry Creek State Park as a good substitution.

Now I am back and probably going to do some work around the house. I guess it all works out. I kinda hope Science beats us for their sake.
