Saturday, December 31, 2005

hidden


There was once this girl who I really liked. In my eye she was the cutest girl in Male Valley even though she denies all compliments. Such a rad chick with her pink slippers, Billabong hoodie that I tried to wear, icicle swords, sweet old chillin dog, screwed up past, and a love for Casa Bonita that won't quit. Unfortunately, she decides to hide behind anything she can so that she doesn't have to face life. She doesn't even want to experience the good times because she is so terrified of the bad times. I hope, for her sake, that someday she can stop hiding and realize that some people can treat her like she is worthy of being treated. If anyone knows who this girl is tell her that.

Thursday, December 29, 2005

Tried to ride

Sean and I went to the lift this morning about 8:40 and it wasn't open due to high winds. The entire mountain was closed. Lame. We waited around until 9:30... Did some shopping and stuff in the meantime. By then the line was crazy so we took off. We wanted to go to Arrowhead, but the roads suck so we flagged that. I just came home and waxed my boards. Working tonight at 4pm. Should be a great time...

Meghan called again today and she is planning to come up tomorrow night and spend the night then ride in the morning. So damn stoked is me.

Riding is gonna be my only life from now until the end of the season with the occaisional trip to Denver to see shows. Jonah is playing tonight and I am bummed as that I can't make it. I was gonna try to switch shifts with someone at work, but figured with all this snow I should just stay here. Don't feel like driving in this crap. It was a hard decision though. It is Jonah...

This week just keeps getting worse

Went riding with the guys this morning... Spencer, Jeff, and Nick. Oh my god. The pow is so sick out there. So many fresh turns. I thought I could ride my cares away, but no chance of that. Ended up running into a rock I was trying to jump off... Don't ask. Then tumbled down the 10-15 foot drop that I wanted to land. By the time I reached the bottom my leg was bleeding and I was in a ravine.

Took a few more runs, but we were all having technical issues with our goggles. I was getting miserable so I took off and headed home. Pics from some cliff drops coming soon.

I forgave once, but it was for something way more minor than what I did. Maybe someday I will be forgiven and I can go back to attacking the fortress. I know I can get through it, but I have am lost and need a map back there.

Powder day

Dan and I went out early this morning with Kim and Jamie. We lost them the second run when we headed into Sundown Bowl and Dan and I got suck in a waistdeep flat pow field. The rest of the day we rocked the trees. We got fresh tracks everywhere. All over Sundown, Sunup, and then on the frontside all over the place.

These are they days I moved here for. Unfortunately, I forgot to bring my camera, but the weather is so windy and snowy it would have been hard to get any good pictures anyway. The forecast for the next few days is looking key. More snow, more snow, more snow.

Sorry. Didn't mean to push it.
