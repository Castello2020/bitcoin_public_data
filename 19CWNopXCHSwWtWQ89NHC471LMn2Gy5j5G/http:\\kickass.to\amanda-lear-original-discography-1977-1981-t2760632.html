Five classic albums with French disco queen and gay icon Amanda Lear ("allegedly" born Alain Louis René Maurice Tapp a.k.a. Peki d'Oslo) including the hit singles "Blood And Honey", "Tomorrow", "Queen Of China-Town", "Follow Me", "Enigma (Give A Bit Of Mmmh To Me)", "Gold", "Run Baby Run", "The Sphinx", "Fashion Pack (Studio 54)", "Fabulous (Lover Love Me)", "Diamonds", "When", "Ho fatto l'amore con me", "Solomon Gundie", "Egal", "Nymphomania", "New York" and "Fever".

Amanda Lear has re-recorded most of her hits from the disco era all through the 80's, 90's and 2000's, but these are the original versions: recorded in Munich between the years 1976 and 1983, produced by Anthony Monn, with the same musicians as on Boney M., Silver Convention and Donna Summer's Munich albums (Keith Forsey, Harold Faltermeyer, Rainer Pietsch, Charly Ricanek, Martin Harrison, Curt Cress, Gary Unwin, Dave King, Les Hurdle, Geoff Bastow, Mats Björklund etc.) and partly at Giorgio Moroder's legendary Musicland Studios - no re-recordings.

Enjoy!
