Back to 1942 movie --> Yi jiu si er (original title)
A great film! A deadly drought in 1942 takes its toll on central China’s Henan province during the war against Japan.
A rather controlled and somber depiction of a dark chapter in modern history, subtly echoing another tragedy that happened 16 years later. Surprisingly un-judgmental and un-sentimental for a historical film recreating despair and lowest possible form of human existence.
The director, FENG Xiaogang, presents the multiple layers of clues and facts that lead to the ultimate tolls almost as-matter-of-factly, leaving the audience putting together the puzzles and drawing their own conclusions, which is a rather clever way of engaging the audience.
Starring: ZHANG Guoli, CHEN Daoming, XU Fan, Adrien Brody, Tim Robbins
Author LIU Zhenyun
Language: both Chinese and English
Feng Xiaogang's disaster epic "Back to 1942" just won BEST FILM AWARD at the 3RD BEIJING INTERNATIONAL FILM FESTIVAL in Beijing, April 23, 2013. It also won Best Visual Effects.
This film received the first ever Tiantan Best Film Award, also known as the Temple of Heaven Awards. It has depth and humanity; it is a universal story and dares to look back at the darkness that Chinese people have had to endure during their dark period...one of their moments of inferno!
