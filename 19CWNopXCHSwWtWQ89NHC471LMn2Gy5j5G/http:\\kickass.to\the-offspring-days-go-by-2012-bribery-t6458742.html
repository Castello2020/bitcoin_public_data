Offspring have been credited with reviving mainstream interest in punk rock in the
United States during the mid-1990s. To date, The Offspring has released eight studio
albums, one compilation, four EPs and three DVDs. They have sold over 34 million albums
worldwide, making them one of the best-selling punk rock acts of all time. Each album
