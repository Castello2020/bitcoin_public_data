Confirmed working!! Did this on a Mac with Leapster 2 (thanks to someone else here for the instructions); First remove battery pack on Leapster 2 & then unscrew the small screw seen inside the battery holding bay. It's a green cover which holds the SD card. You'll only see half the card poking out... I had to wiggle the SD card left to right whilst pulling it, to remove it. Mount the SD card using a USB reader & Mac OS X's Disc Utility. Open "LEAPSTER" to see the folders. It has an 'Apps' directory, drop the individual folders (3 of them) into there, put SD card back into the Leapster 2 & games will appear under Downloaded Games section. Works perfectly.
