Rewd1062 • 19 April 2014, 03:54 Show comment
I know it's an older comment, but if there are others that were wondering, here's some help.

When you've downloaded Calibre, go into 'Add books' and just pick '... single directory', it should be the first option. Then locate your book and when it's been added, go to 'Convert books' (almost right next to Add books). A new window will open and up at the top there will be an input and output format (easy to miss, just look up at the top) and then change the output format to pdf and convert. You will then have a copy of that book in pdf too, it doesn't change the original.
