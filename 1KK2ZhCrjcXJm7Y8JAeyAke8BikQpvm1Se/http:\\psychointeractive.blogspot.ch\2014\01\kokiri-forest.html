Kokiri Forest

Did you ever wanted to walk around in one of your favourite videogames from childhood? Well, now you can, with the Oculus Rift and my recreation of the Kokiri Forest from the Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time :)

You can use WASD + F (Jump) on the keyboard, or a Gamepad.
