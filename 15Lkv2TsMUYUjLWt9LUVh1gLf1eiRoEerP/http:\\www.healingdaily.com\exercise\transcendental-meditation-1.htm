Transcendental meditation is effortless

Transcendental meditation is easy, effortless. In fact, if you are putting any work in it, it may be some other form of meditation, but it's not Transcendental Meditation! The mind is invited to effortlessly dive within, which the mind does spontaneously because the mind's nature is to go toward greater happiness and beauty.

Transcendental meditation is a simple mental technique which allows the mind to gradually experience subtler and subtler states of thought, until the mind transcends even the subtlest state of thought, and experiences absolute silence, BLISS CONSCIOUSNESS.

TM is for you, me, all human beings no matter their religious beliefs or education level. All that is needed is to be instructed in the simple technique, and to practice regularly, morning and evening. We meditate, then go out into the world and become involved in activity. Then we meditate again at night, and perform more activity.

Maharishi explains that it is the cycle of meditation and activity which eventually allows the mind to maintain that state of bliss consciousness at all times, no matter what activity one might be involved in at the time. One could be in the middle of a very chaotic environment, but one's mind remains connected to that sublime field of absolute silence and bliss that is the basis of everything. This activity/meditation cycle gradually brings more and more of the transcendental bliss into our life.
