iPhone Controlled Car - Minivan



iDriver is an iPhone application to remote control a car. It is equipped with various sensors and a drive-by-wire system for unmanned driving. iDriver is an addon to remote control the car with an iPhone. Powered by Appirion UG (http://www.appirion.com).

For further information please visit the official website of the autonomous vehicle at:
http://robotics.mi.fu-berlin.de/pmwiki/pmwiki.php

Or go to http://www.spiritofberlin.eu for more information on "Spirit of Berlin", a completely autonomous vehicle by the Freie Universität Berlin.
