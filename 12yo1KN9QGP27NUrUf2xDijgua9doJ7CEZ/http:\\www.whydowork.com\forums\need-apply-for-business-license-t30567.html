Re: Do I need to apply for a business license?

by joanpeterson on Thu Jan 21, 2010 11:23 am

I agree with Ladybug - it will depend upon the regulations of where you live. Some places require a biz license for any biz, regardless of circumstances.

I would suspect that a cleaning biz would require a biz license as you are performing work for others outside your home. But again - as ladybug mentioned before - check with your local government. If you are required to have a biz license, it is best to just get one. Otherwise,you could face a shutdown or fine for not having one.

You will be able to use the license fee for tax purposes, so ensure you keep a record of any license fees paid to operate your biz.

Re: Do I need to apply for a business license?

by Manolo on Fri Oct 01, 2010 1:40 pm

For any entrepreneur looking to begin their own business they are required to register for a business license. You can pick up a business registration form from your local county occupational tax office. This can be found over the Internet or your local phone book. You will fill out the form, return it and pay a small annual fee from ten to fifteen dollars. This may be a larger fee depending on the city and state of registration.
