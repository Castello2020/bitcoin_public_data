Wouldn't the best way to do it just to be go and take pictures and then submit them to newspapers and magazines? Go to your local corner store and make a note of what magazines they carry or even try the internet (but I think you'll get more there than you can handle at once). Look at what kind of pictures are in them. A friend of mine said the other day that his son is a freelance photographer and that's what he does -- just takes his camera wherever he goes and when he sees something interesting he takes a picture and submits it.

Why wait for a job to come to you when you can simply go to it? My mom picked up some magazines about Canada at a flea market two weeks ago and they're full of pictures that people sent in from across the country and each one of those people received cash or prizes.
