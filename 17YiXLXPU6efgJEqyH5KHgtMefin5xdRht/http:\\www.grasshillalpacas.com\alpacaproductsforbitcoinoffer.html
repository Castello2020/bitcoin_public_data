Survival Socks
We currently offer mens and womens "Survival" socks! These socks are incredible! If you enjoy wool socks, you'll love these. And if you don't enjoy wool socks because they are too itchy for you, then you'll love these socks! Alpaca isn't itchy like wool!
These are 78% pure Alpaca, 20% nylon and 2% lycra. 
 
 

Ankle Socks
In addition to our Survival socks, we are now offering Ankle socks as well! These are a great no-show design perfect for everyday wear. Loop knit sole for extra cushioning, with a reinforced heel, toe, and arch-band for a comfortable fit. Hypo-allergenic, un-dyed and odor resistant. These are 80% pure Alpaca, 18% nylon and 2% lycra.
Treat your feet to the comfort of luxurious alpaca every day!
 
 

All our socks are made in the U.S.A. from fiber produced in the U.S.A.
They are hypo-allergenic, un-dyed and can be machine washed and dried.
