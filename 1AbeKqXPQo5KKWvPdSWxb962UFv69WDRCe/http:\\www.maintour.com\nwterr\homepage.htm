    Northwest Territory Attractions and Activity VIP Discounts

    The Canadian Northwest Territories are located in northern Canada and borders Canada's two other territories, Yukon to the west and Nunavut to the east.

    Prominent geographical features include Great Bear Lake, the largest lake entirely within Canada, Keller Lake and Great Slave Lakes, as well as the Mackenzie River and the canyons of the Nahanni National Park Reserve, a national park and UNESCO World Heritage Site.

    Territorial islands in the Canadian Arctic Archipelago include Banks Island, Borden Island, Prince Patrick Island, and parts of Victoria Island and Melville Island. The highest point is Mount Nirvana near the border with Yukon at an elevation of 9,098 ft. While Nunavut is mostly arctic tundra, the Northwest Territories has a slightly warmer climate and is mostly boreal forest.
