My Top Online Money Making Sites



PTCBox - Earn as high as $0.1 per Ad

Status: Ghost Site
Due to their Paypal and Payza accounts getting limited, PTCBox no longer operates normally.


PTCBox is the newest PTC site owned by the same admin of Matrixmails (a 10-yr paying PTC site).

What makes PTCBox different from Matrixmails or from any PTC sites for that matter? Well, here are the facts:

  1. PTCBox offers a "zero loss" guarantee when you upgrade your account. Members of Matrixmails can attest that the owner keeps his end of the bargain since Matrixmails also offers a guaranteed ROI. In the case of Matrixmails, anyone who is below the guaranteed ROI (125-150%) will have the membership plan extended. As for PTCBox, however, if you fail to earn the upgrade fee within the membership period, you will be refunded the difference of the upgrade fee and the amount you have withdrawn.
  2. The first PTC site where you can customize the type of membership plan you want. You can opt to have your click value increased, have more referral levels, increased referral click value or have them all combined together. With a customized upgrade, you can now opt for the type where you will earn the most. If you're good in referring other people, you can multiply your referral commissions. You're promoting a team build? Then, increase the number of referrals that you can have. Can't refer? Why not increase your ad value up to $0.1? 

Interesting, huh?

How to customize your upgrade:

  1. Click the upgrade link in your back office.
  2. Scroll to the bottom part where it says "Customize Your Own Membership Type".
  3. There are 4 Factors that you can customize:
  • Referral Extra Levels - You can have additional 2 levels of referrals which means you'll have a 3-level referral should you wish to max it out. Each additional level costs $15. This is good for those who are building a team.
  • Personal Earnings Multiplier - Free members earn $0.005 per ad clicked. With this feature, you can add up to 19 additional multipliers. Each extra multiplier costs $5 each. How does it work? Well, let's say you purchased 5 extra multipliers, you will then earn 6 times the regular ad value or $0.03 per ad. If you purchase all 19 additional multipliers, you will earn $0.1 per ad or 20 times of the regular earnings of a free member.
  • Referral Earnings Multiplier - As a free member, you earn 10% of the base ad value ($0.005) that each member clicks or equivalent to $0.0005 per ad. Now, adding this feature you can get up to 100% of the regular ad. An extra multiplier gives you additional 10% earnings from your referral clicks.  Each multiplier costs $5 each. Take note that the multiplier/s will be used on the base ad value which is 0.5 cents.
  • Referral Sign-up Earnings Multiplier - You can get additional 1-4% upgrade bonus with this multiplier. Each extra multiplier costs $10.

And I think we're not done yet because you will also get the following:
  • Free referrals - Depending on the membership type or how you customized your account, you can get anywhere between 1-20 referrals.
  • Advertising tokens which you can use to promote other sites or programs.
