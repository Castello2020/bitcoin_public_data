The Peter Schiff Show -- 2012 May 07 MO

Tom Woods is guest hosting today's show.

Today's first guest is Jacob Sullum, senior editor at Reason Magazine, on why the renewed push for harsher drug laws is full of smoke.

Our second guest is Robert Wenzel, publisher of Economic Policy Journal, on why the New York Fed will probably not be inviting any Austrian economists to speak ever again.
