    Bitcoin miners can use this widget to display the status of their mining efforts on the homescreen.

    Currently supported pools are:
    - deepbit
    - BTC Guild

    Data that can be displayed is:
    - deepbit:
    - hashrate
    - confirmed reward
    - workers alive
    - BTC Guild
    - hashrate
    - total shares
    - confirmed reward
    - Bitcoincharts exchange rates
    - USD
    - EUR
    - MtGox
    - high
    - low
    - vol
    - buy
    - sell
    - last
