Our Mission

To protect and improve the natural, recreational and cultural values of the Clutha Mata-Au River corridor by creating a regional river park and trail.

Option Five

In April, 2009, Contact Energy proposed four dams on the Clutha River, so dam opposition groups proposed OPTION 5, NO More Dams! In May of 2012, all dam plans were cancelled.

Make A Donation

The Clutha Mata-Au River Parkway Group is a not-for-profit organisation with Charitable and Donee Status. Our NZ Charities Commission registration number is CC20398. Donee Status is granted by the Inland Revenue Department (IRD), and allows us to issue official donation receipts.

We rely on donations, membership subscriptions, grants and bequests to carry out our river conservation work.

No amount is too small - any donation is appreciated - thank you!

International Resources

RIVERS NETWORK
A dialogue and knowledge platform, with a global database, sharing information about river basins across the world.

INTERNATIONAL RIVERS
Since 1985, International Rivers has been at the heart of the global struggle to protect rivers and the rights of communities that depend on them. We seek a world where healthy rivers and the rights of local communities are valued and protected. We envision a world where water and energy needs are met without degrading nature or increasing poverty, and where people have the right to participate in decisions that affect their lives.
