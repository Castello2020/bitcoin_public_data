Buy Computer Hardware

Thrift Store

The Free Geek Thrift Store sells refurbished computers and computer-related parts and peripherals.

Click here to visit our price list.

To contact the store, call 604-879-4335 (ext. 2) during our business hours. We are located on the 3rd floor (front entrance) of the Free Geek Vancouver facility.

We will do our best to find what you need, or help you find a solution, but please bear in mind that what is for sale is only what has been donated to us and we do not buy or order parts. We now accept Interac, Visa, Mastercard, and Bitcoin, besides cash.

Are you looking for that hard-to-find or specialized item unavailable everywhere else? Try submitting a request on the form to the right on this page. Please note this is ONLY for specialized, archaic and/or hard-to-find equipment.

Exchange Policy

We recommend that customers research items and compatibility before purchasing -- we will exchange items within 14 days of purchase if they are defective, but not simply because they are incompatible with non-linux operating systems.

Open Help Night is open to anyone seeking help with GNU/linux computers and peripherals, and those looking to make the transition. Please do not expect tech support in the store during regular hours of operation.

We do not offer returns for cash or refunds under any circumstances.

Film Prop & Set Decoration

Are you looking for a specific vintage of computer equipment? Do you need 20 identical monitors? Email sales@freegeekvancouver.org with your requirements.

Wishlist

This wishlist is for archaic and obsolescent equipment that would otherwise be recycled. We'll keep our eyes open for the item, and contact you if it becomes available.
